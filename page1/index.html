In order to stay compliant and make the most of the grant, you will need to have your Google Analytics goals set up properly. We use Google Analytics goals to make conversions. Conversions are the measure of user activity on your website after clicking on an ad. If you don’t know how to set up goals, check out our Google Analytics goals FAQ to learn more.
